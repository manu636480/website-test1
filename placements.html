BMSCCM CAMPUS PLACEMENT

The main objective of the Institution is to develop ethical and innovative leaders in this dynamic and global competitive environment by implementing the theory and practices of what the students have learnt in their curriculum so that they would be able to play leadership roles and contribute to the growth and development of organisations. The Corporate Communication department keeps effective contacts with the firms/companies all over Bengaluru & India as well and is always able to get students a myriad sector of companies for placement opportunities.

PLACEMENT PROCESS:

To get a company to the campus for placements, the corporate communication department of the college establishes contact and finds out the requirement for conducting campus selection. At the initial stage they send their HR team to conduct a survey and decide the common pool centre for campus recruitment. After deciding to hire, the companies ask for the resume of eligible students of final year as per their requirement. They would finalize a suitable date for conducting the campus selection at one of the consortium colleges after consultation with all stakeholders. Normally there is a sequence for the campus recruitment which takes place which is followed by most of the Companies which is as follows –

  • pre-placement Talk
  • Written Test/Aptitude Test
  • Group Discussion
  • Interview – Technical and HR
